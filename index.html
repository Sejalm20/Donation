Giving is receiving

Money invested right

From providing food security through ration supplies to donating sewing machines for fostering skill growth and setting up computer labs at other NGOs for the elderly, the organization seeks to bring a positive impact in each and every aspect of the old age people’s lives that have often been neglected.

Aims and Objectives

To establish our home inmates strength up to maximum strength, so that we can help more senior citizens.
To provide medical facilities & health care to the needy.
To provide nutritious food to the inmates of the center.
To provide shelter & clothing for the residents of the old age home.

Guaranteed to work

We Care foundation has been working towards helping old age people religiously. The NGO, through a network of volunteers, interacts with senior citizens and recognizes their needs and difficulties which are then addressed. With its team of experts, volunteers and social workers, the foundation initiates various projects and programs that are aimed at improving every aspect of the elderly’s lives.

About Us

We Care is a Non Profit Organization that focuses completely and solely on gerontology. Your donations are very valuable to us and all donations will be invested in the betterment of the old buddies that stay with us. Every penny will go towards the mental and physical health of our Aged friends. We provide them with, a place to stay, food and nourishment, medical attention, recreational activities, mental health seminars. We do this through well-planned and comprehensive programmes in health, education, livelihoods, and disaster preparedness and response. We have contacts all around the globe and our investors are based throughout the world.
When a person becomes old then they tend to suffer mental illness and gradual weakness in the body. They are less aware of these mental and physical illness due to the lack of knowledge and information about the health problems as they are not being provided with proper healthcare needs. Thus to provide them with the proper health care treatments and medicines NGO is working effectively.
Although, unfortunately, these disparities are seen in the urban and developed regions where being aware of their fundamental right and knowledge they are turning face from their responsibilities. Due to which the need of old age NGO is significantly increasing in the whole country. Be it medicines, food, treatment and many more, required for their care is given here with utmost satisfaction, comfort and happiness which they need most. The people working here, are wholly committed for their health and other requirements. We Care is dedicated in providing the highest quality compassionate and effective clinical care to those who cannot attain modern specialized health care services, especially in the regions where old people have to go miles for better treatment.
These senior citizens ask for nothing but to spend their last days in peace. Donate your givings to the senior citizen to help provide a life of dignity they deserve.
